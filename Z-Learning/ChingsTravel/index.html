Ching's Travel Journal

Hi! I am Ying Ching or now better known as Szenie (For those who didn’t know, I’ve changed my mandarin name). I am just an ordinary Malaysian who loves to travel like most of you do. For me, writing down the details of travelling is like an act of transforming memories in words.

I started the blog with the objective of “using the simplest English to convey the beauty of travelling”, while at the same time, giving my most sincere point of view. Nonetheless, hope you enjoy your reading here!

Winter in
Innsbruck

I realized that many of the travelers skipped Innsbruck while travelling to Austria. Innsbruck is a city in the Alps, also a place that me and my husband insisted to go while planning out the trip.


Innsbruck is a small city surrounded by magnificent mountains, the view within the city is just super perfect that i would say Innsbruck is my favorite city we’ve visited throughout the journey!
